<a name="136:1">136:1</a> Give thanks to the Lord, for he is good,
for his loyal love endures.

<a name="136:2">136:2</a> Give thanks to the God of gods,
for his loyal love endures.

<a name="136:3">136:3</a> Give thanks to the Lord of lords,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:4">136:4</a> to the one who performs magnificent, amazing deeds all by himself,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:5">136:5</a> to the one who used wisdom to make the heavens,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:6">136:6</a> to the one who spread out the earth over the water,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:7">136:7</a> to the one who made the great lights,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:8">136:8</a> the sun to rule by day,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:9">136:9</a> the moon and stars to rule by night,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:10">136:10</a> to the one who struck down the firstborn of Egypt,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:11">136:11</a> and led Israel out from their midst,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:12">136:12</a> with a strong hand and an outstretched arm,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:13">136:13</a> to the one who divided the Red Sea in two,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:14">136:14</a> and led Israel through its midst,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:15">136:15</a> and tossed Pharaoh and his army into the Red Sea,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:16">136:16</a> to the one who led his people through the wilderness,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:17">136:17</a> to the one who struck down great kings,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:18">136:18</a> and killed powerful kings,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:19">136:19</a> Sihon, king of the Amorites,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:20">136:20</a> Og, king of Bashan,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:21">136:21</a> and gave their land as an inheritance,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:22">136:22</a> as an inheritance to Israel his servant,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:23">136:23</a> to the one who remembered us when we were down,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:24">136:24</a> and snatched us away from our enemies,
for his loyal love endures,

<a name="136:25">136:25</a> to the one who gives food to all living things,
for his loyal love endures.

<a name="136:26">136:26</a> Give thanks to the God of heaven,
for his loyal love endures!
